"Demonstration of how to use system variables to simplify tasks."

"Create a macro to make a character speak a dumb cliche:'

"The cliche macro: Or my name isn't {{}$_speaking_name{}}"

macro cliche
"Or my name isn't {$_speaking_name}!"
endmacro

And here is how it might be used:

char mia
"Well, they had better fall at my feet when I wag my finger."
cliche

char phoenix
"Mia! Please let me try this case! I am ready!"
cliche
